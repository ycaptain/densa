## TL;DR

The turning-point session. Mother's 2026-05-09 disclosure of
the father's pre-death intent-to-repair externally lifts the
guilt-charge that has been fused to the grief — without the
guilt-distortion, the grief integrates into a *clean*, painful,
and meaning-making affect rather than a contaminated, stuck
one. The IFS Manager part ([[inner-protector-stoic]]) is
*re-deployed* unprompted in client's own articulation: same
part, new information, **dialled** the call rather than being
bypassed. Cottage-cheese material is now *tender*; chest band
materially looser. Reyes introduces meaning-reconstruction
language; client generates own meaning-reconstruction via the
Theo / vocabulary / paternal-inheritance frame.

## Narrative reconstruction

Mark opens the session with a phrasing that is itself diagnostic
— *"different-different"* — naming the recursive quality of
shift: he is not asking Reyes whether the change is real, he is
inviting Reyes into a description that already presupposes it.
The clinical move from here is reception, not investigation.

The session's organising event is the 2026-05-09 phone call.
The chain of events Mark articulates is granular: 5 a.m. wake
(unchanged from baseline); making coffee; Theo's drawing left
on the kitchen table the night before, depicting Theo + Mark
plus a third taller figure — Mark's father, drawn into the
family pictures *repeatedly*, Sarah later confirms. Mark stands
in the kitchen at 5:30 with the realisation that *his father is
part of what he is passing to Theo*, and that *what he is
currently passing to Theo includes the pattern of not calling
mother on Saturdays.* He picks up the phone without rehearsing
— a notable behavioural departure from his stated rehearse-
everything baseline.

The call's content matters as much as its occurrence: Mark
discloses the regret-loop (the unresolved fight) to his mother
for the first time; his mother responds with a disclosure of
her own — that in the week before the death, Mark's father
had told her he wanted to drive down and *smooth things over*
with Mark, that he didn't know how to *do that kind of
conversation*, and that *"it's been eating at me."* The
mother has been holding this information for two and a half
months, unsure whether disclosing it would help or hurt; she
discloses because Mark *initiated the call*, which she read as
him being *ready*.

The clinical move that follows is Reyes's somatic re-anchoring
at [06:30]. She asks Mark to attend to his body while saying
the integration *out loud*; the chest band's state is reported
back as *"the loosest it's been in months"* — the hand still
present but *"hands-on-knees"* rather than chest-pressing. The
in-session metaphor that crystallises is *"the foreman went and
sat on a bucket."* Reyes lets it sit for half a minute without
analysing it.

Mark's own articulation at [12:14] is the session's most
clinically generative moment. He volunteers that the call wasn't
*the foreman stepping aside and a younger part picking up the
phone* — it was *the foreman dialling with new information.* He
then enumerates what information the foreman had been receiving
over the prior six weeks:

1. The cottage-cheese contact in session 2 — evidence that
   "letting a little bit of grief through, in the right room,
   doesn't break the system."
2. Chair-work with the foreman in session 3 (2026-04-30, raw
   not in this ingest) — evidence that the protector "could be
   talked to without being fired."
3. Han's medication-deferral validation — *"a doctor in a white
   coat say the way you're working is correct."*

The Theo's-drawing moment is the proximal cue. The cumulative
evidence is what makes the cue *land*.

Sarah's laundry-room reframe at [16:42] is the partner-system
counterpart. *"You weren't protecting me, you were protecting
yourself from being seen by me."* Mark's session-1 framing of
the partner-distance had been "protecting Sarah from how bad
it was." Sarah's reframe is sharper and more accurate. Mark
*does not retreat* — does not go outside for forty minutes,
which he names as his prior pattern. He stays. The behavioural
non-retreat is the clinical datum.

Reyes returns to the cottage cheese at [24:00] as a deliberate
clinical move — *"I want to come back to it now that something
has actually shifted in the system."* The state of the material
has changed: still grief-laden, but the grief is *clean* rather
than *contaminated*. Mark articulates the distinction: *"the
grief by itself is, like — yeah, it's sad. It's deeply sad. It
hurts. But it's *clean*. The guilt was making it dirty."* The
language Mark reaches for here — *clean* vs *dirty* — is
unprompted; it is the affective signature of partial
unburdening.

Reyes introduces meaning-reconstruction language for the first
time: Anderson's *"grief is love with no place to go"* and
Neimeyer's meaning-reconstruction frame
([[grief-as-love-transformed]]). The deliberate sequencing —
*not earlier* — is consistent with the integrative-grief
framework's operating principle that meaning-reconstruction
work imposed before unburdening produces hollow content. At
[30:08] Mark generates an unprompted meaning-reconstruction
of his own: *taking what was unspoken from his father, naming
what was missing without contempt, and choosing to give Theo
more vocabulary than his father gave him.* Reyes names the move
as classic meaning-reconstruction. The continuing-bond practices
Reyes then offers — walk in meaningful places, letter unsent,
talking out loud in the truck — are framed as optional
experiments with built-in permission to decline.

## Working formulation

*(narrative lens)*

The session is the moment a **new narrative** crystallises.
Pre-shift narrative: *"we left it broken. I let my dad die
without making it right. I was constitutionally incapable of
doing what I now regret not doing."* Post-shift narrative:
*"we were both walking toward each other, in our own dumb
ways, and the world didn't give us the time."* Neimeyer's
meaning-reconstruction model predicts that grief integration is
fundamentally a narrative re-organisation: the loss does not
exit the narrative; it is **reframed** within a longer arc that
restores agency and continuity. Mark's spontaneous unprompted
generation of a *forward-directed* meaning fragment — the
Theo / vocabulary frame — is the marker that the narrative
re-organisation is real and not just relief.

Note the explicit *non-contempt* feature: Mark's reframe of
his father — *"he did the best he could with what he had. He
just didn't have the vocabulary"* — is the move that
distinguishes meaning-reconstruction from blame-restructuring.
Without the non-contempt, the narrative would re-organise
*against* the deceased; with it, the narrative integrates the
deceased as a continuing presence (the *continuing-bond*
concept) ([[2026-05-14-session-reyes]] [30:08]).

*(attachment-theory lens)*

The partner-system breach (laundry-room with Sarah) is an
**attachment-style update**. Mark's dismissing-attachment
baseline has been "Sarah knows that I'm not great but not how
I'm not great." Sarah's reframe — *"you were protecting
yourself from being seen by me"* — is itself a piece of
secure-style co-regulation: it names the avoidance without
attack, with affectionate sharpness, and invites Mark to stay
in the room. Mark's non-retreat is an *attachment-style
behavioural update* — the dismissing pattern's reflex (forty
minutes outside) is observed, named, and overridden in real
time. This is the cleanest single-event attachment-update
visible across the four-session arc.

The maternal-contact channel is the parallel attachment update.
Pre-shift, the mother was the attachment figure whose grief
*broke the system*. Post-shift, the mother is the attachment
figure whose disclosure *gives the system new information*.
The relational re-organisation is between Mark and his living
attachment figures (Sarah, mother, Theo), not just between Mark
and his father.

*(eft lens)*

The session's affect-work move at [06:30] is classic EFT:
**embody the new state before naming it.** Reyes asks Mark to
stay in his body while saying the integration out loud, rather
than analysing the integration cognitively. The chest band's
real-time loosening — readable in-room as the protector's hand
loosening — is the *body confirmation* of the unburdening. The
*"foreman sitting on a bucket"* metaphor that emerges is itself
an EFT artefact: the body produced the image, not the
intellect.

The [24:00] return-to-the-cottage-cheese move is sequenced
deliberately. EFT's grief-work literature emphasises that
**re-contacting** previously contained Exile material under
*new structural conditions* (i.e. with the protector willing
to allow contact, with a partial unburdening already achieved)
is what consolidates integration. A single-pass contact
without re-contact under new conditions is fragile; the
re-contact at [24:00] is what makes the integration *stick*.

*(biopsychosocial-4P lens)*

**Predisposing**: the multi-generational containment substrate
is structurally unchanged but now *consciously transmissible* —
Mark's choice to give Theo more vocabulary is the substrate
being deliberately *modified at the next generation*. This is
the first observable intergenerational modification of the
predisposing layer in the arc. **Precipitating**: the
2026-05-09 phone call's mother-disclosure functions as a
*counter-precipitant* — external information that releases the
specific guilt-charge that fused with the grief. **Perpetuating**:
[[avoidant-mother-contact]] materially loosened; partner-
distance materially loosened; [[automaton-work-mode]] still
present at work but loosened at home; the maintenance-insomnia
beginning marginal shift. **Protective**: meaning-reconstruction
articulation now in client's own vocabulary; the **forward**
direction (Theo) is for the first time an explicit organising
intention.
