# Somatic grief containment ("the chest band")

> [!important] Pattern one-liner
> Sustained somatic tightness in the upper chest / lower throat
> region that functions as the body-level expression of the IFS
> Manager part's grief-containment work. Present continuously since
> ~mid-February 2026 (two weeks after the bereavement). The
> body holds what cognition cannot yet metabolise. Loosens visibly
> across the arc — from "a hand pressing down" (sessions 1-2) to
> "the foreman sitting on a bucket" (session 4).

## Phenomenology

- **Location**: upper chest, just below the collarbone, sometimes
  extending up into the lower throat. *"Like a hand closing around
  the bottom of my throat. From the inside."*
  ([[2026-04-02-session-reyes#^[12:08]]])
- **Quality**: pressing / containing rather than crushing or
  stabbing; not cardiac in character (cardiac work-up by PCP was
  negative).
- **Diurnal pattern**: present constantly; worst at the 3:40 a.m.
  waking; modulates with affect contact (intensifies when grief
  material surfaces; softens when in motion or with Theo).
- **Modifiers**: worse with caffeine (client cut back); not
  clearly worse with alcohol; brief relief after aerobic exercise
  (one 4-mile run observed,
  [[2026-04-23-psychiatry-han#^[08:22]]]).

## Mechanism (per [[ifs]] + [[integrative-grief-therapy]])

The band is the **per-second mechanism** by which
[[inner-protector-stoic]] keeps grief material contained. When
the band is in place, daily functioning (work, parenting,
partner-presence) is preserved at the cost of affect access.
When the band loosens (either through somatic anchoring work or
through external information shifting the burden the protector
carries), affect becomes available — but so does the work of
metabolising it.

In meaning-reconstruction language ([[grief-as-love-transformed]]),
the band is the boundary at which love-without-a-place is being
held physically *because* the cognitive container has not yet
been built. Process work + meaning-work together gradually build
the cognitive container; the band loosens as it becomes less
necessary.

## What modulates it

- **Down-modulators (loosen the band)**: brief aerobic exercise;
  somatic-anchoring work with a clinician; external information
  that releases a burden the protector carries (mother's
  disclosure, 2026-05-09); meaning-reconstruction articulation;
  Theo (parental joy).
- **Up-modulators (tighten the band)**: caffeine (mild); thinking
  about calling mother (pre-shift); imagining the unresolved
  conversation with father; the 3:40 a.m. waking context.

## Differential

- **vs. cardiac**: ruled out by EKG + stress test
  ([[2026-04-02-session-reyes#^[12:08]]]).
- **vs. panic attack**: paroxysmal panic episodes absent; pattern
  is *sustained* not *episodic*; no fear-of-dying overlay; no
  hyperventilation.
- **vs. primary GAD somatic**: GAD-7 = 9 (mild-moderate) supports
  some general anxiety contribution, but the *content-tied*
  trigger profile (specifically about father / mother / regret)
  points to grief containment as the primary substrate, with
  GAD-coloured baseline as comorbid contribution rather than
  cause.
